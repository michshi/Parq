What Is Live Outdoors?

We help you achieve your goal of living full time on our country's public lands. We'll turn you into a modern nomad, just tell us how long you plan to be mobile and where you'd like to go - let us do all the planning. We know that it's hard to foresee the future so if your trip plans get interrupted, Live Outdoors is here to help you along the way.

Why Use Live Outdoors?

Whether you want to live off the grid, save some money to buy that dream home, or craving more adventure in retirement, it's time for you to Live Outdoors.
